AUTOMATED TRADING SYSTEM

Discover the On-Chain Scanner from Assassin's Greed Labs, a key part of our Automated Trading System. It identifies promising early-stage projects and key wallets, with customizable alerts to fit your trading goals. Opt in or out of the Ares Sniper system for full strategy control.

ASSASSIN'S GREED SNIPER

Meet the Assassin's Greed Sniper, our swift sniper bot integrated with the Automated Trading System. It snipes launches with precision, guided by bribe features. Set your ETH buy amount and profit targets to match On-Chain Scanner insights for informed trades.

ON-CHAIN ALPHA SCANNER

Explore the On-Chain Scanner, a vital part of Assassin's Greed Labs' Automated Trading System. It targets early-stage projects and key wallets, with custom settings for alpha calls to fit your trade goals. Plus, integrate with Ares Sniper at your discretion for strategic autonomy.

SAFE ANALYSER BOT

Meet the Safe Analyser Bot from Assassin's Greed, ideal for individual and group use. Just use "/a" to get detailed contract insights—market cap, holder count, token fees, liquidity, etc.—for smarter decision-making.

CROSS-CHAIN BRIDGE BOT

Discover our Cross-Chain Bridges, linking multiple blockchains for easy token transfers. Experience the power of DeFi by moving assets across chains effortlessly.

ASSASSIN'S GREED BUY BOT

The Assassin's Greed Buy Bot, within the Labs suite, notifies the Telegram group of new token buys, detailing buyer info, marketcap, transaction, and an image to engage potential buyers.

ETH TOKEN DEPLOYMENT DETECTOR BOT

Stay updated with the ETH Deployment Detector—get real-time alerts for new ETH network tokens. It offers a user-friendly interface with key details like token name, deployer wallet, balance, and checksum.

LIQUIDITY BURN DETECTOR BOT

Assassin's Greed liquidity burned detector provides important information when hunters are looking for safe projects with burnt liquidity.

VOLUME SNIPE DETECTOR BOT

The Assassin's Greed Volume Snipe Detector alerts you to tokens with high sniper activity, hinting at possible alpha projects. Use it to catch opportunities for substantial gains.
